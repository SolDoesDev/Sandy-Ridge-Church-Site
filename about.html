About Us

Who we are

A non-denominational, multi-generational community of Christian believers seeking to follow King Jesus in his movement to change the world for the better.

photo — the gathered church

Come as you are

We’re ordinary people — young families, students, singles, empty-nesters, and everyone in between — united by one thing: a shared faith in Jesus Christ and his good news.

We gather every Sunday to worship God, sit under the teaching of his word, and encourage one another. Through the week we share life in homes, around tables, and in groups across the valley.

You don’t need to have it all figured out. Bring your questions, your doubts, and your whole self — there’s a place for you here.

What does “non-denominational” mean?

It means Sandy Ridge isn’t affiliated with a larger denominational organization, like Baptist, Methodist, or Presbyterian. We’re a local church, governed by our own elders, that seeks to follow Scripture directly rather than a denomination’s distinctives. You can read the specifics of our convictions on our Beliefs page.
