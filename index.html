Our Mission

We exist to glorify God, build up Christians, and reach the lost for Jesus.

What matters to us

Four things at our core

See what we believe →
01

Bible-Based Preaching

We want to go deep in our study of God’s word. Sunday morning sermons at Sandy Ridge Church are usually expository — book by book instead of topic by topic.

02

Strong Families

Raising children to know and love God can be challenging, and we want to come alongside parents to help you as you raise your children to serve Jesus.

03

Community

The Christian life is meant to be lived with others. We desire to be a place where members make lifelong friendships — where meals are shared, and where we celebrate together and grieve together.

04

Truth

We live in a world that is very confused. At Sandy Ridge we want to honor God by holding to his word and his commands, even when they are unpopular.

Who we are

A family following King Jesus together

Sandy Ridge Church is a non-denominational, multi-generational community of Christian believers seeking to follow King Jesus in his movement to change the world for the better.

Whoever you are, wherever you’ve been — you’re welcome here. Come as you are and meet a Savior who changes everything.
