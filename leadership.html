Leadership

Meet our pastors & elders

Sandy Ridge is led by a team of pastors and elders committed to shepherding this church with humility and biblical conviction.

Photo: Lead Pastor

David Whitfield

Lead Pastor

David has served in pastoral ministry for over 15 years and preaches through Scripture book by book. He and his wife Rachel have three children and love hosting the church family in their home.

Photo: Associate Pastor

Marcus Ellery

Associate Pastor, Discipleship

Marcus oversees small groups and discipleship pathways, helping people take their next step in following Jesus. He's married to Priya and they have two kids.

Photo: Youth & Family Pastor

Hannah Cortez

Youth & Family Pastor

Hannah leads our youth ministry and works alongside parents to build faith at home. She's passionate about walking with students through junior high and high school.

Photo: Elder

Tom Reyes

Elder

Tom has been part of Sandy Ridge since its earliest days and serves on the elder board, helping guide the church's direction and care for its members.

Photo: Elder

James Okafor

Elder

James brings a heart for prayer and biblical counsel to the elder team, and serves alongside his wife Grace in the community group ministry.

Photo: Worship Director

Lauren Betts

Worship Director

Lauren leads our Sunday worship team, crafting a gathering that points people's hearts toward God through music and prayer.
