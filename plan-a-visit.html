What to expect

Come as you are

No dress code. Jeans and Sunday best both fit right in.

For your kids

Safe, age-appropriate care and teaching during the service. Just ask a greeter.

The service

Singing, prayer, and a sermon from God’s word — about 90 minutes together.

Parking

Free on-site parking, with greeters directing traffic to the entrance.

Common questions

What should I wear?

Whatever you’re comfortable in. Most people dress casually — there’s no dress code at Sandy Ridge.

Is there a kids’ program?

Yes. We provide safe, age-appropriate care and Bible teaching for kids during the Sunday service. A greeter will help you check them in.

How long is the service?

About 90 minutes, from 10:30 AM to roughly 12:00 PM, including worship, prayer, and the sermon.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just yourself. Bibles are available if you don’t have one, and there’s nothing else you need to bring.

What does “non-denominational” mean?

It means we’re not affiliated with a larger denomination (like Baptist or Methodist). We’re simply a local church seeking to follow Jesus and his word together. Read more on our Beliefs page.

Let us know you’re coming

Optional, but it helps us watch for you and save you a seat.
