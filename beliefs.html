What We Believe

Our beliefs

What we believe shapes how we worship, teach, and love one another. Here's our statement of faith, and the four values at our core.

Scripture

We believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God — our final authority for faith and life. Everything we teach and practice is measured against it.

God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — equal in power and glory.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus is fully God and fully man, born of a virgin, who lived a sinless life, died on the cross for our sins, rose bodily from the grave, and will return again as King.

The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit indwells every believer, empowering us to live for Christ, understand His Word, and bear the fruit of a changed life.

Salvation

We believe salvation is a gift of God's grace, received through faith in Jesus Christ alone — not earned by works, but resulting in a life devoted to Him.

The Church

We believe the local church is God's design for making disciples — a family of believers gathered to worship, grow, and reach their community for Jesus.

Our Core Values

Four things at our core

01

Bible-Based Preaching

We preach expository sermons, working book by book through Scripture so God's Word — not our preferences — sets the agenda.

02

Strong Families

We come alongside parents as the primary disciplers of their children, equipping homes to grow in faith together.

03

Community

We believe faith grows best in relationship — in groups, on Sundays, and in the everyday moments of life together.

04

Truth

We speak the truth of the gospel with conviction and grace, trusting it to do the work only it can do.
