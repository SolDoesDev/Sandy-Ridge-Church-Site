Youth

Students growing in faith, together

Our youth ministry walks alongside junior high and high school students as they wrestle with faith, build real friendships, and learn to follow Jesus for themselves.

Who it's for

Youth Group is open to all students in grades 6–12 — no church background required. We want it to be a safe, welcoming place for students to bring their questions and their friends.

What happens

We gather weekly for worship, games, and a Bible-based talk aimed straight at real life — friendships, identity, pressure, and what it means to follow Jesus in high school. Throughout the year we also host retreats, service projects, and summer camp.

Partnering with parents

We see ourselves as a support to parents, not a replacement. Our goal is to reinforce what's being taught at home and equip families to keep having faith conversations together all week long.
