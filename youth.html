Youth Ministry

Middle and high school can be confusing years. We want students to know they’re loved, to wrestle honestly with their faith, and to build friendships that point them to Jesus.

Our youth gather weekly for teaching, games, food, and small-group conversation — all in a safe, welcoming environment.

photo — students together

What a gathering looks like

Teaching

Age-appropriate teaching straight from the Bible, helping students see Jesus clearly.

Small Groups

Honest conversation in smaller circles, led by caring, vetted adult leaders.

Fun & Food

Games, hangouts, and plenty of food — because friendship matters.
